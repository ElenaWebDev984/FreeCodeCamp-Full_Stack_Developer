Barcelona Women Acceleration Week (BWAW)

Barcelona Women Acceleration Week (BWAW) invites us to reflect on the visibility of women in key areas for the economy. Created to accelerate gender equality to help achieve full participation and equal opportunities for women in the industrial sector locally and globally, panels will address topics such as gender equality in the legal field; women in industry 4.0; the figure of women in the audiovisual environment; parity in large corporations and the public sector; professional growth and retraining; human resources management and the visibility of female talent in programing, pharmaceuticals and health.

Date: Mar 4, 2025 to Mar 6, 2025

Godspeed You! Black Emperor

Godspeed You! Black Emperor began to emerge around 1994 in the minds of Efrim Menuck, Mike Moya and Mauro Pezzente. However, it was between 1997 and 2002 when their first four albums arrived to redefine our ideas, giving another meaning to what we could understand as protest music, with the instrumental compositions of their chamber rock, full of feelings, which could perfectly be the soundtrack of alienation and resistance to the latest capitalism.

Date: Mar 5, 2025 8:00 PM to 9:30 PM

Nightmare Horror Museum

In one of the darkest alleys in the heart of Barcelona you'll find the Nightmare Museum. Located in a 13th century building in El Born, this horror museum serves as an exhibition of terror and an interactive horror experience.

Date: Oct 1, 2024 to Dec 31, 2025

Correfoc de Santa Eulàlia

The Santa Eulàlia fire festival is a feast for your senses with drums, the smell of gunpowder and the light of the sparks. Barcelona's fire beasts and devils will gather in Plaça Reial to begin their correfoc (fire run) on their way to take their fire to Plaça de Sant Jaume.

Date: Feb 8, 2025
